If you wish to contribute changes to this project, you will need to sign our
contributor license agreement. You may find the agreement in the doc/ folder;
use IGA-saveload-individual-contributor-agreement.docx if you are contributing
as an individual, or IGA-saveload-corporate-contributor-agreement.docx if you
are contributing on behalf of a company. Information about where to send your
signed agreement is contained within those documents.
